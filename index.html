Welcome to THREE js

THREE.js is a Library for drawing 3d Graphics in the browser with Web GL and JavaScript

What are the components of THREE js?

Scene & lights

A scene must have one or more lights to make shapes visible. The scene is like a container that holds your objects cameras and lights.
